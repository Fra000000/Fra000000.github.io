Describe the scope of their work in detail.

We were able to work in true partnership with Flower and they became an extension of our digital team. For this project we engaged their team with the initial client brief and walked through the brief on a call with the Flower team.

The Flower team also checked in throughout the campaign to make adjustments to the media to improve performance.
